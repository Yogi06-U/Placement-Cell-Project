About Placement Cell

Campus recruitment is a process through which the corporate (employer) organization recruits the required talent pool from the academic campuses. The selection process takes place in the final year of a programme for students of both undergraduate and postgraduate courses. Over the years, campus recruitment has assumed greater significance in the minds of several stakeholders. The major stakeholders of the recruitment process are institutes, companies, students, and parents.
